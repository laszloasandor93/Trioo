About TRIOOO hookah

TRIOOO (originally known as Hookah Trio) has been a popular hookah bar in Târgu Mureș since 2021, offering special hookah flavors and refreshing drinks. We provide high-quality service and a pleasant environment where our guests can relax and enjoy the unique hookah experience.

Through our Instagram and Facebook pages, we share our latest promotions, new flavors, and events that attract the local smoking community. Follow us too!
